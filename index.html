Smart Backwashing Safe Water System for Plateau Pastoral Areas

Durable Purity for Nomadic Life

Our innovation targets the core issue of the persistent "safe drinking water gap" in the western plateau pastoral regions of China. Through four consecutive years of field-based philanthropic work since 2021, we observed that herders at remote summer grazing sites, lacking basic infrastructure, are forced to consume untreated surface water with high turbidity and contaminants like Echinococcus eggs, leading to high rates of waterborne diseases.

Our product is the first solution globally designed specifically for this context. Centered around a granted utility model patent, it innovatively achieves "one-button, dual-action" smart backwashing and complete pipeline drainage, simultaneously solving the key challenges of water purification in high-altitude pastoral areas.

The Problem

Core challenges in plateau pastoral drinking water safety

Water Quality Issues

In remote northwestern China, residents in pastoral, border, and disaster-prone areas relying on scattered surface water commonly face turbid water and parasitic contamination. In high-altitude areas, parasite eggs cannot be killed, leading to various illnesses.

Existing Solutions Fail

Residential water purifiers require city electricity, impacting portability and maintainability. Current portable water purifiers often suffer from blockage and freeze-overs from turbid and cold water. Boiling water wastes fuel, is expensive, and cannot clean aqueous chemicals.

Infrastructure Gap

The "remote grazing sites" in plateau pastoral areas represent an "infrastructure vacuum" for safe drinking water. Herders lack access to safe, convenient drinking water during summer pasture production and daily life. Centralized water supply systems are impractical due to seasonal migration and high costs.

Our Solution

Systemic solution designed for extreme environments

Patented Dual-Solenoid Backwash

Features a patented double-solenoid-powered backwash mechanism, using remaining water to wash residue, eliminating clogs and secondary contamination. Combined with solar-powered dual-mode electric power, multi-parameter sensing, and IoT connectivity.

Off-Grid Solar Power

Solar-powered dual-mode electric power system ensures operation without grid electricity. Residential water purifiers lack needed electricity and are hard to carry. Portable ones have no automatic backwashing to extend filter lifespan.

Extreme Environment Adaptation

Housing uses ABS+PC composite material with IP54 rating. Key pipelines are food-grade silicone, ensuring reliability and freeze protection (via complete drainage) in environments from -20°C to 60°C.

Smart Sensing & Control

Centered on a microcontroller (ESP8266/STM32), integrates flow, temperature, and TDS sensors for real-time monitoring. System intelligently calculates usage and auto-triggers the backwash cycle, employing algorithms for efficient low-power management.

Unique Value Proposition

Residential water purifiers lack the needed electricity and are hard to carry. Portable ones have no automatic backwashing to extend filter lifespan, making both unsuitable. Our product features automatic backwashing powered by the sun all in a small box, making it portable, reliable, and eco-friendly.
